About

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Huzaib, a Data and Insights Analyst currently working in BBC, one of the largest media houses in the world.

I am a data-driven business and insights analyst with over four years of experience in transforming raw data into actionable insights that fuel strategic decision-making. My expertise spans across SQL, Python, Power BI, Tableau, and ETL pipeline automation, enabling me to design robust data solutions that optimize performance, enhance predictive modeling, and streamline workflow automation. I have a strong track record of developing interactive dashboards that provide real-time analytics, KPI tracking, and data visualizations, empowering stakeholders to make informed business decisions efficiently. Additionally, I specialize in automating ETL pipelines and implementing CI/CD workflows, ensuring seamless data integration, transformation, and deployment. By leveraging data-driven methodologies, I help businesses enhance operational efficiency, identify growth opportunities, and make proactive, evidence-based decisions that drive success.
